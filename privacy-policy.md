# Privacy Policy for What-Else

**Last updated: February 20, 2026**

## Introduction

What-Else ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS application.

## Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your data is:
- NateDevJ (Sole Developer)
- Email: feedback@what-else.app
- Location: Germany

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your data based on:
- **Contract performance** (Art. 6(1)(b)): To provide the app's core functionality (task management, sync, notifications)
- **Legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f)): To maintain and improve the app
- **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)): For optional features like notifications (you can revoke consent at any time in iOS Settings)

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Tasks**: Title, emoji, color, interval settings, scheduled days (for weekly/monthly/yearly tasks), due dates, reminders, notes, completion history (including On-Time Rate records)
- **Categories**: Category names, colors, and sort order
- **Settings**: Theme preferences, notification settings, appearance mode, sound preferences
- **Pro Subscription**: Purchase status and subscription type (managed entirely by Apple)

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- No analytics or usage tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No location data
- No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- No browsing or search history
- No contacts or address book data

## How We Use Your Information

- **iCloud Sync**: Your tasks, categories, and settings are synced across your devices using your personal iCloud account
- **Notifications**: Local notifications are scheduled on your device for task reminders and daily overviews
- **Widgets**: Task data is shared with the widget extension using App Groups (local to your device)
- **Siri Shortcuts**: Task data is made available to the Shortcuts app for voice commands (local to your device)

## Data Storage & Retention

- **Local Storage**: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- **iCloud**: If you're signed in to iCloud, your data is automatically synced using Apple's CloudKit framework to your personal iCloud storage
- **No Third-Party Servers**: We do not operate any servers. Your data never passes through infrastructure we control
- **Retention**: Your data persists until you delete it manually within the app or delete the app. iCloud data persists according to your iCloud settings

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple iCloud / CloudKit**: For data synchronization (subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/))
- **Apple StoreKit**: For in-app purchase processing (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
- We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services

## Data Security

- Your data is protected by Apple's device encryption and iCloud encryption
- All iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We do not have access to your data at any point
- Your data never leaves Apple's ecosystem

## Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access** (Art. 15): View all your data within the app at any time
- **Right to Rectification** (Art. 16): Edit any task or setting within the app
- **Right to Erasure** (Art. 17): Delete individual tasks, categories, or all data through the app's settings. Deleting the app removes all local data
- **Right to Data Portability** (Art. 20): Export your task data using the app's export function (Settings â†’ Data Management)
- **Right to Restrict Processing** (Art. 18): Disable iCloud sync in iOS Settings to stop data synchronization
- **Right to Object** (Art. 21): You can stop using the app and delete your data at any time
- **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Revoke notification permissions in iOS Settings at any time
- **Right to Lodge a Complaint**: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory data protection authority, in particular in the EU Member State of your habitual residence

Since all data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account, you have full and direct control over your data at all times.

## Children's Privacy

What-Else does not knowingly collect data from children under 16 (in accordance with GDPR). The app does not require any personal information to function. The app is rated 4+ on the App Store.

## In-App Purchases

- **What-Else Pro**: Subscription and lifetime purchase options are available
- All payment processing is handled by Apple through StoreKit
- We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information
- Subscription status is verified locally through Apple's StoreKit framework
- All transactions are subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) and App Store terms

## International Data Transfers

Your data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Apple may store iCloud data in data centers outside the EU. For information on Apple's data transfer practices, please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
